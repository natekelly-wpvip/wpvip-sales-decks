Subscriptions, Reimagined

Your Magento is critically out of date and Darwin cannot activate digital subscriptions in real time. You need a subscriber to get immediate access when they sign up, just like the Wall Street Journal.

WooCommerce sits on the same WordPress installation as your sites. No separate codebase, no integration gap. When a subscriber checks out, their membership is activated instantly. Stripe stays as your payment gateway.

And for your digital editions, WordPress PDF viewer plugins replace eMags with a mobile-friendly, gated archive. VIP does not charge for media bandwidth, so you eliminate that cost entirely.

⚡

Instant Digital Access

WooCommerce memberships activate the moment a subscriber completes checkout. No delay, no manual provisioning, no Magento dependency.

🔓

Flexible Paywalls with A/B Testing

Hard registration gates, metered access, or freemium models. Test which approach drives the most conversions, per publication or per content type.

📖

Digital Magazine Archive

Replace eMags with a mobile-responsive flipbook plugin. Your full archive back to 2018, gated by subscription tier, with click-through advertising intact.

🏢

Institutional Access

IP-based institutional subscriptions for the libraries and school districts that make up your subscriber base. Manage it all from one backend.

The Build: How We Get There

You have no web developer on staff and limited appetite for a long-term agency retainer. WordPress VIP's Automattic for Agencies (A4A) program is designed for exactly this situation.

We match you with a vetted agency partner who specializes in publishing migrations. They handle the design and development. We handle the infrastructure, code review, and technical guidance. Your team focuses on content.

1

Agency Introduction

We present 2-3 gold-certified agency partners with publishing migration experience. You evaluate fit, portfolio, and pricing.

2

The agency works with your team to define the build: site architecture, K4 integration, WooCommerce setup, paywall configuration, blog migration plan.

3

Design, Build, and Migrate

The agency builds your three sites. WordPress VIP reviews all code, manages infrastructure, and provides technical guidance throughout.

4

Launch and Handoff

Your editorial team takes the wheel. WordPress VIP handles hosting, security, and updates. Agency support scales down to what you actually need.
