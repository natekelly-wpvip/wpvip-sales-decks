Subscriptions, Paywalls, and Digital Replicas

Your publications depend on subscription revenue. WordPress VIP supports the full spectrum of paywall strategies, from metered access to hard paywalls with institutional licensing.

Nearly every publisher we serve runs some form of paywall or subscription program. The architecture is proven at scale across news, trade, and specialty publishers.

And for your digital magazine replicas, WordPress plugins can replace eMags with a mobile-friendly, fully gated reading experience that serves your entire archive.

🔓

Flexible Paywall Models

Metered, hard, freemium, or hybrid. Configure per-publication or per-content-type. Gate reviews while keeping news open, or vice versa.

🏢

Institutional Access

Support for IP-based institutional subscriptions, perfect for the libraries and schools that make up your subscriber base.

📖

Digital Magazine Replicas

Registration and Conversion
