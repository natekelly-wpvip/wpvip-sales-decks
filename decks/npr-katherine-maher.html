Risk Assessment

Brightspot:
The Uncomfortable Truth

Vendor Instability

PE-owned by Tailwind Capital. Approximately 218 employees and $24M in revenue. Back-to-back layoffs: 10% followed by 15% in consecutive rounds.

Glassdoor: 2.7/5 stars. Only 32% of employees recommend the company.

Talent Scarcity

Built on Java and the proprietary Dari framework. Finding developers with this niche skill set is orders of magnitude harder than PHP/WordPress talent.

WordPress: 1.3M+ developers globally. Brightspot/Dari: estimated hundreds.

Proprietary Lock-in

All customizations, content models, and workflows are locked to Brightspot's proprietary platform. If you leave, your code doesn't come with you.

Gartner review: "An unfinished idea you spend a lot of money trying to make work."

Hidden Costs

Customers report paying for Brightspot's own bug fixes. Same code across departments incurs multiple licensing charges. Training is an additional cost.

41% of Brightspot customers spend over $100K/year. ITQlick pricing score: 4.4/10.
