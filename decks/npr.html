Where Brightspot Falls Short

An honest look at the platform constraints holding NPR back.
🔒

Proprietary Lock-in

Java-based, closed-source architecture limits customization and ties you to a single vendor's roadmap and timeline.

👥

Limited Ecosystem

A fraction of WordPress's 60,000+ plugins and massive developer community. Fewer integrations, fewer solutions out of the box.

⚡

No Native AI Integration

AI capabilities require third-party bolt-ons. No native Model Context Protocol (MCP) support for intelligent content workflows.

📈

Scaling Complexity

Handling traffic spikes during breaking news requires significant custom infrastructure work vs. a managed global CDN.

📝

Rigid Editing Experience

No real-time collaborative editing. Editors work in silos, creating version conflicts and slowing editorial velocity.

💰

Higher Total Cost

Licensing, specialized Java developers, and custom infrastructure drive up TCO compared to an open-source managed platform.
