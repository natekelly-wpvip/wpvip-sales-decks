Case Study: TIME

TIME
Digital Media & Publishing

Challenge

TIME needed a modern content platform that could handle massive traffic spikes during flagship editorial moments, improve editorial velocity, and support a revenue model tied directly to content volume and audience reach.

Solution

A comprehensive re-platforming of TIME.com using WordPress VIP and the Gutenberg Block Editor, with Google Cloud and Next.js on the front end. Reusable Patterns and Blocks revolutionized how the editorial team manages content, enabling rapid layout changes for breaking news.

Result

TIME tripled content creation, achieved 100% site uptime, and saw an 82% surge in traffic. When Taylor Swift was named Person of the Year, TIME.com handled over 100,000 requests per second without breaking a sweat.
