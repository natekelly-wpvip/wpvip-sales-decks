The Arc-XP Reality

🏦

Owned by The Washington Post / Jeff Bezos

Arc-XP is not an independent company. Its roadmap, investment, and survival depend entirely on decisions made by WaPo leadership and Bezos himself.

📉

Washington Post Revenue and Readership in Decline

WaPo has shed hundreds of millions in revenue and lost significant readership over the past several years, triggering multiple rounds of mass layoffs.

✈️

Development Offshored to Bulgaria

Engineering resources have reportedly been moved overseas, raising questions about product velocity and the support quality Arc-XP customers can expect going forward.

Proprietary Lock-In, No Open Source Safety Net

Arc-XP is a closed, proprietary platform. If the vendor exits the market or pivots, your content and workflows have nowhere to go.
